Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do I need a membership to eat at the restaurant?

No! Our performance restaurant is open to the public. Everyone is welcome to enjoy our macro-friendly meals.

Can I try the gym before joining?

Absolutely. We offer a free day pass so you can experience everything Iron Fuel has to offer before committing.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions. Training packages are included in Elite memberships or available a la carte.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a large free parking lot directly in front of the facility. Bike racks are also available.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Just bring a valid ID, comfortable workout clothes, and athletic shoes. We provide towels and water stations throughout the gym.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes. We don't believe in long-term contracts. You can cancel your membership with 30 days notice.
