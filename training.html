Training Facilities

Everything you need to build strength, endurance, and performance.

Our Equipment

Premium gear for every type of training.

🏋️

Free Weights Zone

Dumbbells ranging from 5 to 150 lbs, Olympic barbells, EZ curl bars, and specialty bars. Multiple squat racks, bench press stations, and deadlift platforms with competition-grade bumper plates.

🦾

Machine Floor

Full selection of plate-loaded and cable machines targeting every muscle group. Includes chest press, lat pulldown, leg press, hack squat, and isolation machines for precise muscle targeting.

🏃

Cardio Deck

Treadmills, ellipticals, rowing machines, assault bikes, and stair climbers. All cardio equipment features personal screens with entertainment options to keep you motivated.

🔗

Functional Training Area

Battle ropes, kettlebells, medicine balls, plyo boxes, and suspension trainers. Open turf space for sled pushes, farmer's walks, and dynamic movement training.

💪

Strongman Corner

Atlas stones, log press, yoke walk, and tire flip stations. For those who want to train like competitors and build real-world functional strength.

🧘

Stretch & Recovery

Dedicated stretching area with foam rollers, massage guns, and mobility tools. Recovery is part of the process, and we give you the space to do it right.

Amenities

Everything you need before and after your workout.

🔐

Secure Lockers

Full-size lockers available for all members. Store your gear, valuables, and work clothes while you train. Day-use lockers are free; private rentals available monthly.

🚿

Showers

Clean, private shower stalls with complimentary towels, body wash, and shampoo. Get ready for work or your day without heading home first.

💨

Changing Rooms

Spacious locker rooms with benches, mirrors, and grooming stations. Hair dryers and basic toiletries provided.

💧

Hydration Stations

Filtered water stations throughout the gym. Fill up your bottle before, during, and after your workout.
